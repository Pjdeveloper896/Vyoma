🌍 About the AUU

The Asha–Udo Universe is Vyoma’s Indo-Nigerian storytelling multiverse. From superheroes and cosmic battles to heartfelt rom-coms and comedies, AUU is a growing hub of interconnected worlds.

✨ Mini-Universes

🦸 Superhero Universe

Captain Azad, Narsh, Fireking, Nehe, Pman, Rider, Y the savior, and the superhero team Silent Flame.

😂 Comedy Universe

Lighthearted Indo-Nigerian humor, campus life, and cultural fun.

💕 RomCom Universe

Stories of love across Delhi and Abuja — bridging two worlds.

🌌 Mythic & Cosmic

Narshima fights, Igbo spirits, and Vedic-tech cosmic battles.
